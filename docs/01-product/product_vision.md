# Product Vision - Family Cashbook

## 1. Problem

Couples often track household money in fragmented ways:
- Notes app, spreadsheets, chat messages, and memory.
- No single trusted family balance.
- Low visibility into spending trends and debt obligations.

Result:
- Financial discussions become reactive.
- Budget overruns are discovered too late.
- Savings goals are difficult to maintain.

## 2. Solution

Family Cashbook provides a shared digital notebook for 2-member families:
- Fast transaction logging.
- Always-visible family balance.
- Clear budget, debt, and fund tracking.
- AI support that is helpful and gated by data quality.

## 3. Target Users

Primary users:
- Married couples or partners managing one household budget.

Secondary users:
- Small families needing lightweight finance control without full fintech complexity.

Core constraint:
- One family space has exactly 2 members sharing all household finance records.

## 4. Core Value Proposition

- Clarity: know where money goes and how much remains.
- Speed: add transactions in seconds.
- Transparency: both members share one source of truth.
- Progress: track budgets, debt payoff, and savings goals in one place.

## 5. Product Differentiation

- Notebook-like UX, not an enterprise finance interface.
- Balance-first model backed by wallet-aware transaction logic.
- Purpose-built for 2-member family collaboration.
- AI features are gated and explainable instead of noisy automation.

## 6. Success Metrics

Adoption and usage:
- Daily active family spaces.
- Weekly active families.

Behavior and retention:
- 7-day and 30-day retention.
- Median daily logs per active family.
- Quick Add success rate.

Financial outcomes:
- Budget breach reduction over time.
- Fund goal attainment rate.
- Debt overdue rate reduction.

System quality:
- Transaction latency.
- Sync delay between devices.
- Data consistency incidents.

## 7. Vision Horizon

3 months:
- Stable MVP with wallet, expense/income/fund, and reliable sync.

6 months:
- Full core pillars including transfer, debt, and budget alerts.

12 months:
- AI-assisted planning, recurring workflows, and multi-platform expansion.
